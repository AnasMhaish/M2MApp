# Secure D2D Caching with Blockchain

Recently proposed method of caching and distribution of packets among devices has been accepted by the
community as a cost-effective method of transferring especially
audio-video data among mobile users. Another newly established
technology Blockchain has been successfully applied to decentralized financial systems like Bitcoin. The proposed work presents
a scheme to provide security and integrity of network packets
in caching and distribution system by employing Blockchain
technology with a lightweight consensus method. The scheme
also allows peers in the networks to make correctly addressed
inquiries.
